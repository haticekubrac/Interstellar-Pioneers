2017 Total Solar Eclipse

During the total solar eclipse, the Sun’s corona, only visible during the total eclipse, is shown as a crown of white flares from the surface.

2021 Partial Solar Eclipse

A partial solar eclipse is seen as the sun rises to the left of the United States Capitol building, Thursday, June 10, 2021, as seen from Arlington, Virginia.

2022 Total Lunar Eclipse

The Moon is seen during a total lunar eclipse, Tuesday, Nov. 8, 2022, at NASA’s Kennedy Space Center in Florida.

Image of the Moon taken by Expedition 13

A partial lunar eclipse is featured in this image photographed by an Expedition 13 crewmember on the International Space Station as the station flew over the southern Indian Ocean.

Flower Moon Lunar Eclipse 2022

The Flower Moon lunar eclipse over NASA’s Michoud Assembly Facility in New Orleans is shown from the initial partial eclipse to totality in a composite of seven images shots.
